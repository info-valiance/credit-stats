Marketing Dashboard - Customer Insights

Of all online transactions, 60% happen during the business hours, that is between 9am to 5pm with the average order value being the highest on Fridays.
Impulsive categories like apparels and computer/mobile accesories dominate the online market accounting for 50% of all online purchases.
Groceries category has shown high growth in the past year, accounting for 11% of all online purchases as compared to 4% in the last financial year.
